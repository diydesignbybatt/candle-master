# Privacy Policy for Candle Master

**Last Updated: January 27, 2025**

## Introduction

Candle Master ("we," "our," or "us") is an **educational mobile application** designed to teach users about stock trading concepts, candlestick patterns, and technical analysis through a simulated trading environment. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

**Important:** Candle Master is a trading simulator for **educational purposes only**. No real money is involved, and the app does not provide financial advice or facilitate actual securities trading.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information from Google Sign-In

When you sign in with Google, we receive:
- Your name
- Your email address
- Your profile picture

This information is used solely to identify your account and personalize your experience within the app.

### 2. Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored **locally on your device**:
- Game progress and statistics
- Trading simulation history
- App preferences and settings
- Selected theme preferences

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively for:

| Purpose | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Authentication** | To sign you into the app securely |
| **Personalization** | To display your name and save your progress |
| **Educational Experience** | To track your learning progress in the trading simulator |

We do **NOT** use your information for:
- Advertising or marketing
- Selling to third parties
- Profiling or behavioral tracking
- Any purpose unrelated to the app's educational function

---

## Data Storage and Security

| Data Type | Storage Location | Security |
|-----------|------------------|----------|
| Account info (name, email) | Firebase Authentication (Google Cloud) | Encrypted, secured by Google |
| Game progress | Your device (localStorage) | Protected by device security |
| Trading history | Your device (localStorage) | Protected by device security |

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

---

## Third-Party Services

Candle Master uses the following third-party services:

### Firebase Authentication (Google)
- Purpose: Secure user authentication
- Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### Stooq (Historical Stock Data)
- Purpose: Provide historical stock price data for educational simulations
- Note: No personal data is shared with this service

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media tracking services.

---

## Children's Privacy

Candle Master is an educational app suitable for users learning about financial markets. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

| Right | How to Exercise |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Access your data** | View your profile in the app settings |
| **Delete your data** | Sign out and uninstall the app, or contact us |
| **Withdraw consent** | Sign out at any time through the app |

To delete all your data:
1. Sign out from the app
2. Clear app data from your device settings, or
3. Uninstall the application

---

## Data Retention

- **Account data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Local game data**: Stored on your device until you clear it or uninstall the app
- **After deletion**: All data is permanently removed

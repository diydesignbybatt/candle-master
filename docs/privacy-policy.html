Privacy Policy

Candle Master - Trading Education App

Educational Purpose Only
Important: Candle Master is a trading simulator for educational purposes only. No real money is involved, and the app does not provide financial advice or facilitate actual securities trading.

Last Updated: January 27, 2025

Information We Collect

1. Information from Google Sign-In

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile picture

This information is used solely to identify your account and personalize your experience within the app.

2. Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Game progress and statistics
  • Trading simulation history
  • App preferences and settings
  • Selected theme preferences

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up.

How We Use Your Information

Purpose Description
Authentication To sign you into the app securely
Personalization To display your name and save your progress
Educational Experience To track your learning progress in the trading simulator
We do NOT use your information for advertising, marketing, selling to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to the app's educational function.

Data Storage and Security

Data Type Storage Location Security

Children's Privacy

Candle Master is an educational app suitable for users learning about financial markets. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Your Rights

Right How to Exercise
Access your data View your profile in the app settings
Delete your data Sign out and uninstall, or contact us
Withdraw consent Sign out at any time through the app

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Local game data: Stored until you clear it or uninstall
  • After deletion: All data is permanently removed
